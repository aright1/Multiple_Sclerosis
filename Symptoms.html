Symptoms

Common symptoms include:
  1. Numbness or tingling
  2. Electric-shock sensataion
  3. Lack of coordination
  4. Trouble walking
  5. Weakness
  6. Loss of vision
  7. Dizziness
  8. Fatigue
  9. Memory issues
  10. Mood swings
  11. Slurred Speech

There are no set symptoms nut many people may experience very different symptoms, and
these symptoms aren't consistent as they can change over course of the disease.
